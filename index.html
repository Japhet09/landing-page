Here are the Big five game animals

The lion (Panthera leo) is a large, carnivorous feline found in Africa. It has a short, tawny coat; a tufted tail; and, in the male, a heavy mane around the neck and shoulders.

The leopard is considered the most difficult of the Big Five to hunt because of its nocturnal habits

The African bush elephant (Loxodonta africana) is a very large herbivore with thick, almost hairless skin; a long, flexible, prehensile trunk; upper incisors forming long, curved, ivory tusks; and large, fan-shaped ears

The black rhinoceros (Diceros bicornis) is a large herbivore with two upright horns on its nasal bridge.

The African buffalo (Syncerus caffer) is a large horned bovid. It is the only animal among the Big Five that is not on the “endangered” or “threatened” list
